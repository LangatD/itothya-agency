OUR SERVICES

Here are the services Itothya General Agencies Ltd. offers

Debt Recovery/Collection

This is our main area of expertise. We undertake two major categories: commercial and consumer debt recovery.

Investigation/Skip Tracing

We use field-based collections and investigations to track down debtors. We also serve notices for legal purposes.

Process Server

Our team offers process serving services, providing professional and court server services for the processing of documents.

Debt Surveillance

We monitor debts for companies and can advise them on changing circumstances.

Consultancy

Our team advises clients on how to set up debt collection policies and systems.

Reconciliation

involves comparing financial records from different sources to ensure accuracy and consistency.

Asset Searches

Our team searches for assets using their knowledge of public information databases.
